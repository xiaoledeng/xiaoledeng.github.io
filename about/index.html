About

My name is Xiao-Le Deng. I received the Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctorate degrees from School of Geodesy and Geomatics of Wuhan University in June 2013, June 2016, and June 2019, respectively.

My current research mainly focuses on applications of gravity and magnetic field modeling (e.g., gravitational curvatures (引力曲率), magnetic curvatures (磁力曲率)) in geoscience.
