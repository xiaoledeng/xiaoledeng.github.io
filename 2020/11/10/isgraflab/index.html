isGrafLab: Irregular Surface GRAvity Field LABoratory

2020-11-10 10:00

1. Introduction

isGrafLab (Irregular Surface GRAvity Field LABoratory) is a modified version of GrafLab that allows accurate and fast computation of functionals of the geopotential at dense grids on irregular surfaces such as the Earth's surface.
