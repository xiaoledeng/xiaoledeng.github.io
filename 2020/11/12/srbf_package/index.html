SRBF_package: spherical radial basis functions

2020-11-12 09:00
#Researchsoftware #Matlab #Fortran #Python

1. Introduction

SRBF_package is a collection of a few routines for gravity field modelling with band-limited spherical radial basis functions (SRBFs), allowing both analysis and synthesis of the gravitational field.

Website:

  • Bucha/#SRBF_package
  • http://edisk.cvt.stuba.sk/~xbuchab/SRBF_package/SRBF_package.zip

2. Reference
