GrafLab: GRAvity Field LABoratory

2020-11-09
#Researchsoftware #Matlab

1. Introduction

GrafLab (GRAvity Field LABoratory) is a MATLAB-based routine for computing functionals of the geopotential up to ultra-high degrees and orders (tens of thousands and beyond).
