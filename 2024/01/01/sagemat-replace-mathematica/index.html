SageMath is a free open-source mathematics software system licensed under the GPL. It builds on top of many existing open-source packages: NumPy, SciPy, matplotlib, Sympy, Maxima, GAP, FLINT, R and many more.

“Creating a Viable Open Source Alternative to Magma, Maple, Mathematica, and MATLAB”

  • SageMath is the most popular Web-based, Windows, Mac & Linux alternative to Mathematica.
  • SageMath is the most popular Open Source & free alternative to Mathematica.
